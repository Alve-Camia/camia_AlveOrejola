### Q3: As the school year (S.Y. 2025-2026) ends, if you plan to continue working on the project, what would be the next steps?
<ul>
  <li>1. Revisit the "Known Issues" found in the Project Documentation, and address those issues if feasible.</li>
  <li>2. If possible, gather comments and suggestions from peers or people from the intended audience, which in our case is the students and teachers of PSHS-EVC. Otherwise, analyze the code from a user's perspective and identify areas of improvement.</li>
  <li>3. Evaluate gathered comments in terms of relevancy, overall effectiveness in solving the issue/problem, and clarity of the suggestions' intention.</li>
  <li>4. Implement the "approved" comments/suggestions.</li>
  <li>5. If there is available time, and there are no other school assessments/projects to do, repeat Steps 2-5.</li>
</ul>
