## APACE: Assistive Project Attendance Considering Efficiency

This year in Computer Science, I learned a lot about programming and problem-solving. Some lessons were difficult for me, especially iterative structures and arrays, but they helped me become more patient and improve my logical thinking skills. Even though I struggled at times, I still learned how important practice and perseverance are in coding.

It was challenging but rewarding because I was able to apply what I learned and see how programming can be used in real-life situations. Overall, this subject helped me grow both in my skills and confidence in computer science.
